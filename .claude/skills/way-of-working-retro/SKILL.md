# Way-of-Working Retro

## When to use
The replay pack is done. Turn the lived experience into improvements to the loop itself — the framework is the product here.

## Inputs
- Replay pack.
- Team feedback and friction points.
- Where blockers and rework happened.

## Steps
1. Identify the hand-offs that worked vs the ones that leaked.
2. Map where AI genuinely accelerated vs added noise.
3. Propose keep / change / drop actions on skills and gates.
4. Recommend changes to the skill catalogue or guardrails.

## Output — artifact as proof
WoW retro: keep/change/drop list, hand-off health, concrete skill/gate changes with owners.

## Human gate
Leads decide which process changes to adopt.

## Guardrails
- AI proposes lessons; humans decide what changes.
- Lessons are grounded in the replay evidence, not opinion — cite the moment.
